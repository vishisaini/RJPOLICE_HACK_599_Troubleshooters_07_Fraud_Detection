About Us

This web application helps identify potential credit card fraud in real time using machine learning. Simply enter the details of a transaction, and the system will instantly tell you whether it’s likely to be legitimate or fraudulent. Why this matters: Credit card fraud causes huge financial losses globally. Our tool helps detect unusual patterns based on trained models and protects users from suspicious activity. 🛠 How it works: You input transaction details (like amount, time, etc.) The data is processed and sent to a trained ML model A prediction is returned instantly — “Fraud” or “Not Fraud” presented to you by: Vishi Saini Vineet Kumar Jangid Rishabh Jain Rajat Kumar Keshav Madhav.
